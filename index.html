General MetaMask Transactions APR
Can I make payments directly from an exchange?
NO. Please do not deposit from exchanges. You will need MetaMask to intereact with the smart contract.
Do I need to "Pump Fuel" AKA "Compound" exactly 24-hours apart?
NO! This is just a rough estimate for best compounding rates. You may proceed with your own time-frame as long as its at-least once-a-day.
How is the 8.00% daily calculated?
The interest rate is entirely variable and may fluctuate to adjust the smart contract's health. You may see days with higher rewards and other days may be lower rewards. This is strictly done to create longevity within the smart contract.

To have the highest chance of reaching 8.00% daily, users must practice "Pumping Gas" aka "Compounding" daily for 6 days a week before having a chance to reach maximum rewards. Theoretically, if you withdraw daily, your reward rate will decrease. If you continue to "Pump Gas", you may eventually have higher reward rates.
Where do I download MetaMask from?
Please visit https://metamask.io/download/ to read more about how to install and download MetaMask.
How do I add AVAX to MetaMask?
Please visit https://chainlist.org/ and searh for AVAX. You must have MetaMask pre-installed to your browser. Then connect your wallet to automatically add the network. Alternatively, please visit https://www.avax.network/ for more information.
What Chain is AVAX on?
This project runs on AVAX-C. When using MetaMask, please ensure you deposit to AVAX-C and NOT AVAX-X.
"I deposited more AVAX into the dApp and my pending rewards are gone/missing? Where did they go?"
Do not worry. Your rewards are auto-compounded once you deposit new funds into the smart contract.
How do referrals work? Where are my referred payments?
Referral rewards are automatically sent to your pending rewards section of the dApp. You can claim the referral rewards there anytime.
My transcation is stuck, HELP!
If you see your transaction pending, you have the option to click "Speed Up" and enter a higher gwei amount in the fees section. Another ideal solution would be to set advanced gas optimization by clicking "Settings", then "Experimental", and turn on "Enable Enhanced Gas Fee UI". You can switch your gas setting to "Aggressive" to faster transactions. There are fees within MetaMask, please use cautiously.
What is a variable interest rate?
VIR (Variable Interest Rate) is sometimes called an "adjustable" or "floating" rate, which is a type of interest rate that fluctuates over time because it is based on an underlying benchmark interest rate or index that changes periodically. Compared to a "Fixed Interest Rate", a "Variable Interest Rate" means that the reward rate can fluctuate and change relatively to factors within the smart-contract.
How are the daily reward rates affected?
This is simple. The three main reasons the rewards rate fluctuate are as follows:

1) Contract Balance (at the time of being rewarded)
2) Frequency of daily deposits and withrawls from other users and yourself 3) Overall market conditions (price of AVAX) fluctuating
What are "Attendants" and how are they correlated with rewards?
"Attendants" are your work-horses. Think of them as your own personal army. They each own part of the smart-contract. The "Attendants" that you hire, represent a portion of the smart contract size. AVAX deposits turn into "Attendants". The more "Attendants" you have, the higher your daily reward if compounded.

Staking Pool Adventure

The mission is simple. You are on a journey and need "Fuel" along the way. You'll need to "Pump Fuel" to ensure you don't ever run out! If you run out of fuel (Withdraw) too often, you may find yourself slowing down. Fuel Station is locked staking pool with variable interest rates to help you get across country. AVAX is deposited and turned into "Attendants". "Attendants" earn up to 8.00% variable APR daily on your deposit and future compounded rewards. All deposits are LOCKED, meaing no dev/team member has access to the main locked wallet where rewards are transacted from.

Attendants: Your intial investment essentially turns your AVAX into "Attendants" (Yes, the ones that pump gas for you!). Each attendant earns you a certain % of the smart-contract based on your initial/compounded deposit.

Note: Your initial investment will not be able to be withdrawn at any point. Its locked into the reward pool when you "Hire Attendants". You are only able to withdraw your earnings.

Pump Fuel: Throughout your adventure, you will have the option to "Pump Fuel", which takes the reward you've generated, and compounds back into the Smart Contract.

Example: John deposits 1 AVAX. In 24-hours, John is rewarded up to 8.00% on his deposit. John now has the option to withdraw the 8.00% earned, OR "Pump Fuel" and compound to base his rewards off 1 AVAX + earned rewards compounded *0.08%.

What if the funds start to deplete?
Before the funds begin to deplete, the variable APR will kick in resulting in lower rewards, essentially slowing down time and attempting to prevent loss. 50% of the Withdrawl/Deposit fees can be used to inject AVAX back into the smart contract. The rest of the % will be used for marketing and development.
